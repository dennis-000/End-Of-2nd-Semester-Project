Wrestling

Develop strength, agility, and mental toughness through the ancient sport of wrestling.

  • Learn various wrestling styles: Freestyle, Greco-Roman, and Folkstyle
  • Improve physical fitness and body awareness
  • Build self-confidence and discipline
Join Wrestling

Taekwondo

Master the Korean art of self-defense and improve your overall physical and mental well-being.

  • Learn powerful kicks and striking techniques
  • Enhance flexibility, balance, and coordination
  • Develop self-discipline and respect
Join Taekwondo

Judo

Experience the gentle way of Judo and learn efficient techniques for self-defense and competition.

  • Master throwing techniques and ground grappling
  • Improve balance, coordination, and body control
  • Learn the principles of maximum efficiency with minimal effort
Join Judo

Volleyball

Join our dynamic volleyball program and experience the thrill of this fast-paced team sport.

  • Develop essential skills: serving, passing, setting, and spiking
  • Enhance teamwork and communication abilities
  • Improve cardiovascular fitness and hand-eye coordination
Join Volleyball

Rock Climbing

Challenge yourself physically and mentally with our exciting indoor rock climbing program.

  • Learn proper climbing techniques and safety procedures
  • Build upper body strength and improve flexibility
  • Develop problem-solving skills and overcome fears
Join Rock Climbing

Ultimate Frisbee

Experience the fast-paced, non-contact sport that combines elements of football, soccer, and basketball.

  • Improve cardiovascular endurance and agility
  • Develop throwing and catching skills
  • Learn strategies and enjoy a sport that promotes fair play
Join Ultimate Frisbee
