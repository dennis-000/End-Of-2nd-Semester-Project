Vision:

The SRC Sports Academy of Pentecost University envisions nurturing athletes and leaders who excel in sports while upholding Christian values and academic excellence.

Mission:

The SRC Sports Academy is committed to developing well-rounded student-athletes through rigorous training, academic support, and spiritual growth, creating champions on and off the field.

Core Values:

  • Discipline: Instilling self-control, commitment, and perseverance in all aspects of sports and academics.
  • Teamwork: Promoting collaboration, respect, and mutual support among athletes, coaches, and the community.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in performance, training, and academic achievement.
  • Faith: Upholding Christian principles and spiritual growth as the foundation for character development.
  • Leadership: Cultivating leadership qualities that inspire positive change within and beyond the sports arena.
